Burritos Recipe

Description

This recipe is about making a burrito.

You can also make two burritos.

Ingredients

  • Corn tortillas
  • Chicken breast
  • BBQ powder
  • Red beans
  • Chedar cheese
  • Salsa sauce
  • Sour cream

Steps

  1. Put BBQ powder on chicken
  2. Cook chicken
  3. Add beans
  4. Shred cheese
  5. Put everything in tortillas
